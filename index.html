Welcome to Our Website!

Bara Kartika is an Art home industry that produce artificial tiger and other species of leopard. The artificial tiger and leopard are made of Carbon fiber for the body and leather goat for the skin. We make these artificial to reduce hunting of protected animals. We profide many different kind of expression and movement of the artificial Tiger and leopard. Let us keep the preservation of protected animals.
